PUBLIC SAFETY AUTHORITY // INTERNAL CONTROL NETWORK

Bureau of Cognitive Integrity

Official Cognitive Safety Authority

Overview Structure NPCs Hooks Secrets

Faction Overview

Public Face

The BCI presents itself as Meridian Spire’s guardian against cognitive contamination, trauma spirals, dangerous memory instability, and illegal memory technology.

Actual Truth

The BCI protects the edited version of history. It uses surveillance, records manipulation, medicalized memory correction, propaganda, and tactical force to keep the city stable and obedient.

Player-Safe Read

Citizens know the BCI as stern but necessary. Licensed PIs know the BCI can freeze cases, revoke access, lock terminals, and make evidence disappear.

GM Warning

The BCI should feel lawful before it feels evil. Their scariest power is not violence; it is making everyone agree the violence was necessary.

Territory

Civic Core, Cognitive Health Clinics, BCI Processing Towers, patrol zones, black-site annexes, and emergency response corridors.

white BCI letteringred enforcement lightsgreen terminal scansclean geometric armorofficial seals

Internal Structure

Directorate

Policy, citywide control, elite coordination, classified continuity orders.

Internal Security

Leak suppression, erased case files, black-bag warrants, evidence laundering.

Patrol Division

Street presence, checkpoint authority, PI interference, public compliance.

Enforcer Corps

High-risk tactical response, raids, forced extractions, memory seizure operations.

Cognitive Health Division

Therapeutic memory correction, compliance medicine, post-incident reconditioning.

Public Trust Office

Propaganda, citizen advisories, controlled crisis narratives.

Choir of the Unforgotten

Memory Rebels / Grief Movement / Underground Resistance

Overview Structure NPCs Hooks Secrets

Faction Overview

Public Face

The BCI describes the Choir as dangerous agitators who radicalize the grieving and spread illegal memory material.

Actual Truth

The Choir believes forgotten people have been murdered twice: once in life and once in memory. They fight to recover names, stories, and proof of erased lives.

Player-Safe Read

Citizens know graffiti, hacked screens, and whispers: “The Choir remembers.” PIs hear that they can find witnesses nobody else can.

GM Warning

The Choir should not be clean heroes. They are right about the crime, but desperation can make them reckless and dangerous.

Territory

The Dregs, runoff tunnels, slum sectors, pirate relay stations, hidden memorial rooms, abandoned civic shelters.

Truth Division

Unedited History Cell / Purple-Signal Hackers

Overview Structure NPCs Hooks Secrets

Faction Overview

Public Face

Most citizens do not know Truth Division exists. BCI files describe them as conspiracy terrorists spreading corrosive historical falsehoods.

Actual Truth

Truth Division wants the unedited past exposed, even if the revelation destabilizes Meridian Spire. They are less emotional than CotU and less patient than the Red Archive.

Player-Safe Read

Players first experience them through purple flashes, terminal warnings, and messages like “you aren’t ready.”

GM Warning

Truth Division should be allies who make the party nervous. They value truth more than comfort, safety, or timing.

Territory

Hidden servers, dead terminals, ghost forums, compromised civic relays, old academic networks, abandoned data vaults.

The Red Archive

Forbidden Records / Elegant Intelligence Brokers

Overview Structure NPCs Hooks Secrets

Faction Overview

Public Face

Officially, the Red Archive does not exist. Unofficially, people whisper that someone kept the records the city tried to bury.

Actual Truth

The Red Archive preserves forbidden records, erased names, and pre-edited history. They believe truth is a weapon and should not be handed to everyone.

The Archive is not heroic. They protect truth by controlling it. They may help the party while still manipulating them.

Continuance Initiative

Neural Continuity / Memory Transfer / Elite Survival Science

Overview Structure NPCs Hooks Secrets

Faction Overview

Public Face

Continuance claims to treat neurological decay, trauma loss, identity collapse, and memory deterioration through advanced continuity research.

Actual Truth

Continuance is one of the few groups that knows memory transfer is real. Its leadership is obsessed with preserving identity beyond death, especially for the powerful.

GM Warning

Territory
